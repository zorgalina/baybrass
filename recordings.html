Sound The Bells! GRAMMY NOMINATED -- BEST SMALL ENSEMBLE PERFORMANCE!
American Premieres for Brass

Released March 8, 2011

       The Bay Brass presents a showcase of American music both rousing and moving with these first recordings of recent works by American composers John Williams, Michael Tilson Thomas, Morten Lauridsen, Bruce Broughton, Kevin Puts and Scott Hiltzik. Sound The Bells!, recorded in 5.1 Surround Sound at Skywalker Ranch, is available both as a hybrid SACD disc and as a download.
