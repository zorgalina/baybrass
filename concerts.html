A Brass & Organ Christmas
The Bay Brass with Grace Cathedral's Aeolian-Skinner Pipe Organ
Grace Cathedral, San Francisco, CA
Monday, December 12, 2022, 7:30 PM

       "Experience the Bay Area's finest brass musicians in concert with Grace Cathedral's acclaimed 7,466-pipe Aeolian-Skinner organ.  The acoustics of the cathedral nave are a perfect match for the brass instruments combined with the cathedral's acclaimed great organ which was installed in 1934. A musical highlight of the year!"

Please join us for our annual Brass & Organ Christmas concert at Grace Cathedral on Monday December 12, 2022 at 7:30 pm. Come fill your ears with holiday cheer provided by The Bay Brass. This year's concert will feature several new arrangements and many traditional favorites. Hope to see you there!
